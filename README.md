
# CRM24

## Overview
**CRM24** is a business ready sales, service and customer engagement platform that is installed and configured for your organisation in less than 24 hours, powered by Microsoft's Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement platform.

Your business will be ready to sell and service your customers without the huge costs involved in a long and expensive customised software deployment through extensive and expensive consulting agreements. Our reconfigured solution is already customised to fit most organisation's standard business process models, and comes pre-packed with a host of generically customisable features, built according to industry best practice 
standards, and allows further extension and full scalability intact.

It is a launch pad for continuous growth of customer relationships, profits and synchronisation of your organisation. It is further enhanced with built-in automated workflows for internal and external communication.
